My master's thesis at IUST. Hybrid manufacturing/remanufacturing systems face uncertainty
on multiple fronts simultaneously:

- The **quality** and **quantity** of returned products,
- **Production and reprocessing times**,
- Demand for both new and remanufactured products.

I developed a multi-objective robust optimization model that handles these sources of
uncertainty jointly, enabling strategic capacity and sourcing decisions that remain
feasible across a budgeted uncertainty set. The two working papers on my CV (on hybrid
planning and on green closed-loop supply chains) extend this work.
