Built a discrete-event simulation in Arena to model a multi-stage transportation system.
Outputs included utilization profiles, queue-length distributions, and sensitivity of
throughput to arrival-rate and service-time perturbations — used to locate the binding
bottleneck rather than guessing it from headline averages.
