Designed and ran an empirical study using Structural Equation Modeling (PLS-SEM in
SmartPLS) to test how **green trust** mediates the relationship between perceived product
attributes and willingness-to-pay a premium for sustainable technology. Includes survey
design, construct validation, and mediation analysis.
